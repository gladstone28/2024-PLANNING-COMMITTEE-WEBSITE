Frequently Asked Questions

What are the church service times?

Our services are held every Sunday at 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM.

You can get involved by joining one of our many groups or volunteering for events. Please contact our office for more details.

Do you have programs for children and youth?

Yes, we offer a variety of programs for children and youth of all ages. Check our 'Youth Programs' section for more information.
